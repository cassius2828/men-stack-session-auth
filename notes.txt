Authorization
///////////////////////////
________________________________________________________

Definition: Verifying a user’s identity.
________________________________________________________

	•	Ensures users are who they claim to be.
	•	Users create an account with a username and password.
	•	Users provide the correct password to access features or data.


________________________________________________________

Data Security: Ensures users interact only with relevant data.
________________________________________________________

	•	Protects sensitive data from unauthorized access.
	•	Prevents unauthorized modifications.
	•	Maintains integrity and security of the application and user data.
